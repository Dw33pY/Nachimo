About NACHIMO

Professional Contracting & Maintenance

NACHIMO CONTRACTORS LTD is a fully integrated contracting company based in Nairobi, Kenya. We bring together skilled professionals across every trade — plumbing, electrical works, landscaping and garden design, painting and decorating, cleaning services, general maintenance, and civil engineering.

From residential renovations to large-scale commercial projects, we deliver quality workmanship with reliable service at affordable prices. One company, one point of contact, one standard of excellence across every service we provide.
