The Beginning

It Started With a Refusal to Compromise

In 2015, our founder saw a gap in Kenya's contracting industry — too many companies offering individual trades with no accountability. Projects were delayed, communication broke down, and clients were left frustrated.

NACHIMO CONTRACTORS LTD was founded on a simple idea: bring every trade under one roof, one management structure, one standard of excellence. Whether you need plumbing, landscaping, electrical work, or all of the above — one call, one team, one guarantee.

Today, with over 500 completed projects across 18 counties, we're one of Kenya's most trusted integrated contracting companies.
